How to Find E-Books
How to Find E-Books Locating e-books in the UW Libraries collection involves two steps: searching for material using relevant words and phrases, and refining the search results to display only eBook titles. 

         1.Type your title or topic into UW Libraries Search.
         2. Click on Availability: Available Online and Resource type: eBooks, then click on Apply Filters.
         3. Select an e-book title and click on the title for the item.
         4. You can then click on the link available.
